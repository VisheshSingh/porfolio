SEPTA Rail Fare Calculator Widget

An interactive widget for calculating SEPTA Regional Rail fare prices.

This Septa Rail Fare Calculator was part of the design challenge at one of the company's I was interviewing for.
The task involved combining business logic with a new intuitive interface.

Basically, When you take regional rail in and out of the city, the fare price is affected by where you purchase the ticket, when you ride, and how far you travel. All the information related to the fare price were dumped into a JSON file from the SEPTA's official website

At the Front-end, I designed the widget seen in the screenshot by using HTML and CSS. Users have complete freedom to change the selection of zones from different options; whether they are travelling on weekdays or weekends; if they would like to purchase tickets onboard or station kiosk and the number of rides they need to ensure a comfortable journey.

Based on the user's of when there are riding; information float from right to left to give detailed description about their selection. Behind the scene this information is grabbed from the JSON file

Users are also made aware of any discounts or special pricing based on the number of trip-tickets they select. Like in this screenshot, the traveller gets a better deal for the same amount of price.

The application is completely responsive and lets users calculate their rail fare price right from thei mobile devices. I achieved this responsive layout by using media queries
